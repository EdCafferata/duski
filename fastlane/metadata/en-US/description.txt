Duski helps you fall asleep faster with sounds generated 100% on your own device — no recordings, no streaming, no rights risk. Start by choosing who the device is for (baby, child, teen, adult or senior): Duski automatically adjusts the content and a safe volume limit.

MIX YOUR OWN SOUND
Pick individual sound layers and blend them together, each with its own volume:

• Noise — white, pink, brown, grey and blue noise
• Nature — rain, waves, wind, campfire and a babbling stream
• Body & baby — heartbeat, womb sounds, breathing, shushing (shhh) and a hairdryer/white-noise-machine sound
• Other — singing bowl, fan, train, ticking clock, airplane cabin, driving in a car, and classical pieces by Bach, Pachelbel, Satie and Debussy (our own arrangements, inspired by these long public-domain compositions)

SLEEP TIMER
Set how long the sound plays (15 to 90 minutes) — Duski stops automatically so your battery doesn't drain while you sleep.

SCREENSAVER
Pick a calming animation that matches your category: starry sky, counting sheep, candle flame, cloud cover and more.

SAFE FOR EVERY AGE
Baby mode includes a built-in volume limit — research shows that standalone white-noise machines are often too loud at full volume. Every age group gets content suited to it.

HONEST ABOUT THE SCIENCE
Duski is based on research from AASM, WHO and Harvard Medical School, and we're just as clear about what isn't backed by evidence — no pseudoscientific claims.
